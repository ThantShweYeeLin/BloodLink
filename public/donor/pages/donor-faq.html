Donor FAQ

Answers to common questions about donating with BloodLink.

How often can I donate blood?
Most donors can give whole blood every 56 days. Check your eligibility page for your personalized next date.
What should I do before donating?
Stay hydrated, eat iron-rich foods, and bring a photo ID. Avoid heavy exercise right before your appointment.
Can I cancel or reschedule an appointment?
Yes. Go to Appointments, choose your booking, and tap Cancel or Reschedule. If you need help, contact your drive organizer.
How do rewards work?
You earn badges for milestones like first donation, streaks, and total donations. Certificates can be downloaded from the Rewards page.
What happens to my test results?
Your donation is screened according to guidelines. Results and statuses appear in Donation History when available.
How do I update my profile info?
Open Profile to edit contact details, address, and password. Save changes to keep your records current.
